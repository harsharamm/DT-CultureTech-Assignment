# Methodology

## Objective
Identify Indian specialty manufacturing companies matching DeepThought’s Federer ICP profile.

## Research Process
1. Selected Hyderabad as the target city due to its strong biotech, defence, and specialty manufacturing ecosystem.
2. Focused on:
   - Defence electronics
   - Specialty biotech
   - Specialty chemicals
3. Used:
   - Official company websites
   - BSE filings
   - MCA filings
   - DSIR records
   - LinkedIn
4. Evaluated companies using the 6 Federer criteria:
   - Manufacturer
   - India-based
   - Differentiation
   - Technical decision maker
   - Growth sector
   - Growth signals

## AI Usage
AI tools were used for:
- Structuring company data
- Summarizing findings
- Organizing scoring format

Manual verification was done for:
- Revenue bands
- Certifications
- Ownership structure
- Technical leadership
- Manufacturing presence

## Key Learnings
- Many biotech companies are actually CRO/service companies and not manufacturers.
- Ownership structure is important; PE-owned companies often fail the Federer profile.
- Defence and specialty chemical sectors showed the strongest growth signals.
